Our Story

SwayaDev Labs was founded with one belief businesses shouldn't waste human potential on repetitive work. We saw companies losing thousands of hours every month to manual processes, copy-pasting data, chasing follow-ups, and fixing preventable errors.

So we built a team that combines deep AI expertise with real business understanding. We don't just automate we redesign how your business operates, replacing manual bottlenecks with intelligent systems that work 24/7 without breaks, errors, or delays.

Today, SwayaDev Labs helps businesses across industries save time, cut costs, and scale faster using custom-built AI automation systems.
